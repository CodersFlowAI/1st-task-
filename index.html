Welcome to Cyber World Organisation

The Cyber World, or cyberspace, is more than just the Internet. It refers to an online environment where many participants are involved in social interactions and have the ability to affect and influence each other.This is an Non-Profit Organisation, Started by Sumit Oneness & Piyush Kaushik. People interact in cyberspace through the use of digital media. Examples of cyberspace interactions are: Create media, share media and consume media.

Characteristics of Cyber World:

  • Creative and “Out-of-the-box” Thinking
  • Thrive in “Hands On” Work Styles
  • Constantly Learn and Evolve
  • A Mile Deep in Technical Knowledge
  • Ability to Think Quickly About Solving New Challenges
